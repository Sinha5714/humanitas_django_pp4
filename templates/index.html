Humanitas

Act To Connect

Our Vision

At Humanitas, we envision ourselves as a worldwide hub connecting individuals with diverse assistance needs. Whether it's local community support, legal guidance, or aid organizations addressing critical human rights issues, we aim to serve as the central point for accessing help in any situation. In the years to come, our goal is to ensure that every person, regardless of their location, can turn to Humanitas whenever they require assistance. We see ourselves as a valuable intermediary, lightening the load for institutions and companies by connecting people in need with the right resources. By doing so, we foster a more efficient approach to addressing the concerns and needs of humanity.

Our Mission

Across the world, countless opportunities exist to find assistance for every problem that arises. However, if a person doesn't know where or how to seek help, they may never receive the support they truly need. This applies to everyday worries, managing illnesses, dealing with bullying, and even grappling with profound challenges like suicidal thoughts. When individuals face problems, their performance and well-being are inevitably impacted to varying degrees. This, in turn, has far-reaching consequences for humanity as a whole, including social security systems, workplaces, and beyond. At Humanitas, our mission is to provide every individual with the help they require. This assistance can take the form of connecting them with existing support resources such as aid organizations, associations, psychotherapists, medical professionals, legal advisors, and more. Alternatively, it may involve engaging in a conversation with one of our specially trained "Green Angels." Through Humanitas, we strive to ensure that no one is left without the assistance they need, empowering individuals to overcome obstacles and regain their well-being.
