How it started!

My husband and I met in 2018. At that time, he was an serving in Army. He had signed a contract for life. Termination excluded. Moreover, the army refuses to recognize the legalization of homosexuality in his country. He was not allowed to be gay. To have a future together, we had to find a way for his discharge. We contacted over 100 human rights lawyers, organizations and associations worldwide within eight months. Some we met in person, including world-renowned lawyers. Most of the requests went unanswered, the rest could not or would not help us.

Finally, after many refusals and disappointments, desperation led us to a military lawyer. This could also become dangerous. But we were lucky and met an LGBT friendly lawyer who knew how to deal with indian law. 2.5 years later, my husband was released after a long ordeal. We were successful only because we brought the following: Patience
Willpower
Togetherness
Knowledge
Strength
Self-confidence
Money

About Us

Our Vision

Humanitas acts as a global filter function for people in need to existing help. We only connect when necessary and to the right address of help. Trained staff can recognize whether a request requires referral to an aid organization or if help can already be provided in advance with a professional consultation.
