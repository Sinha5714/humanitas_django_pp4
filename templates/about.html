About Us

Humanitas acts as a global filter function for people in need to existing help. We only connect when necessary and to the right address of help. Trained staff can recognize whether a request requires referral to an aid organization or if help can already be provided in advance with a professional consultation.

Our Story

We are a couple. Two men from two countries that couldn't be more different. India and Germany. In 2018, fate brought us together. Before that, each lived an individually happy life. One was a an officer in miliary and a department manager on a cruise ship fleet. We met by chance in Kochi, South India and fell in love the second we saw each other. Unsuspecting that the years that followed would turn our lives upside down and present us with challenges far beyond what was imaginable to allow us to live a life of togetherness. For in the beginning, it looked as if both of us would be denied a life together. Our path brought us many hurdles, which we all managed to overcome sooner or later, because we had certain prerequisites such as courage, assertiveness, money, persistence, support and each other. At some point we asked ourselves the question, how do people master or even solve a life-threatening situation, who for various reasons do not have the necessary prerequisites?! An extensive research in the family, friends, - & acquaintance circle brought only one answer: They give up.
This sobering realization was the birth of "Humanitas".
