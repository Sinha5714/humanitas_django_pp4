About Us

What We Do!

Humanitas acts as a global filter function for people in need to existing help. We only connect when necessary and to the right address of help. Trained staff can recognize whether a request requires referral to an aid organization or if help can already be provided in advance with a professional consultation.

Our Story

We are a couple, two individuals hailing from vastly different countries â€“ India and Germany. In 2018, destiny intervened, bringing us together. Prior to that moment, each of us led separate lives filled with happiness. One served as a naval officer in military, while other held a managerial position within a cruise ship fleet. By a stroke of luck, our paths crossed in Kochi, South India, and the instant we laid eyes on each other, love ignited. Little did we know that the years to come would unravel our lives, presenting unimaginable challenges that would test our resilience and jeopardize our dreams of togetherness. Initially, it appeared as though our longing for a life intertwined would be thwarted. Our journey was fraught with numerous hurdles, each of which we managed to conquer, armed with essential attributes such as courage, determination, financial stability, perseverance, unwavering support, and the unwavering bond we shared. Along our arduous path, we found ourselves pondering a critical question: How do individuals navigate or overcome life-threatening situations when they lack the necessary prerequisites for success? Extensive research within our circle of family, friends, and acquaintances yielded a disheartening answer: Many simply surrender to their circumstances. This poignant realization served as the catalyst for the birth of "Humanitas".
